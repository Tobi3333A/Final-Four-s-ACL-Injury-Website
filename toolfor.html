Who Should Use This Tool?

Empowering Athletes to Stay Strong, Smart, and Safe

Every athlete—no matter the sport or level—deserves to train confidently without the constant fear of injury. That’s why this tool was created: to help young athletes in the U.S. understand and reduce their personal risk of ACL injury.
ACL injuries are one of the most common—and most preventable—setbacks in sports. They can end seasons, affect scholarships, and even change careers. But with the right awareness and data, athletes can take control of their knee health before problems start.

Who This Tool is For

  1. High School and Collegiate Athletes
    If you play soccer, basketball, volleyball, football, or any sport involving jumping, cutting, or rapid direction changes, this tool is built for you. It helps you identify whether your current training habits or recovery routines might be increasing your risk of ACL injury.
  2. Coaches and Athletic Trainers
    Coaches can use the tool to monitor team-wide injury risk levels. With access to insights about training load, rest balance, and recovery patterns, you can make smarter practice plans that protect players and maintain peak performance.
  3. Parents of Young Athletes
    Parents can use the results to understand their child’s physical demands and learn how to support them with proper rest, nutrition, and medical attention when needed.
  4. Rehabilitation and Health Professionals For physical therapists or sports medicine specialists, this tool provides data-driven insights that can guide rehabilitation plans, monitor progress, and identify athletes who may need additional support.

Why It Matters

This isn’t just another fitness tracker.
It’s a personalized, research-based risk assessment system designed to help you:

  • Understand your ACL risk level
  • Learn how training, rest, and recovery affect your joints
  • Receive customized recommendations to prevent injury
  • Connect with licensed professionals if your risk level is high
Whether you’re trying to make varsity, compete in college, or just stay healthy, this tool gives you the knowledge and guidance to play harder—and smarter.

Takeaway

If you’re an athlete who trains seriously and wants to protect your body while improving performance, this tool is for you.
It’s more than just numbers—it’s about understanding your body, improving safely, and keeping your passion for the game alive.
